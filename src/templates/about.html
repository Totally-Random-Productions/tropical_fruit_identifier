WHAT IS THE TROPICAL FRUIT IDENTIFIER?


The Tropical Fruit Identifer is a classifier that identifies Caribbean fruits. Currently, the model is trained to identify oranges, lemons, cocoas and papayas. The model is not 100% accurate and so we collect images to further train it. In the future, we hope to train our model to identify a broader variety of fruits.

We collect fruit images that are uploaded to the application as to further expand our sample of fruit images used to train the model. By doing this, we hope to be able to train the model enough to be applicable to real life scenarios, such as identifying a farmer's produce without human assistance and automating processes pertaining to the procurement and transportation of produce.

By uploading an image to our application, you agree to allow us to use the image for further training of the Tropical Fruit Identifier model. This model can then be used in an unlimited number of ways in the future. If you have no problem with that, what are you waiting for?
